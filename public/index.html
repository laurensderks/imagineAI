About this project

This tool was created by school teacher Laurens Derks using vibe coding. It was designed to demonstrate how teachers can create custom teaching resources that respond to the unique needs of their students and learning environments.

Laurens was inspired to develop the idea after visiting the Microsoft office in North Sydney with the Year 6 cohort, where students explored how AI is creating new opportunities for learning and creativity. AI has the potential to elevate creativity, and this tool aims to give students access to some of that magic by showing how AI can act as a creative partner.
